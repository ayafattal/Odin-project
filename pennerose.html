Penne Rosé


The perfect blend of tomato and cream sauce, this pasta with pink sauce is one of our favorite dishes to make and comes together so quickly!

Creamy sauced penne rosa is a simple pasta meal. Penne rosa can be prepared in one pot as a whole. It's the perfect weeknight meal, this quick and easy pasta dish with tomato cream sauce. It is satisfying, savory, and hearty. It's therefore impossible to go wrong with this spicy tomato cream sauce penne pasta dish if you're looking to make something creamy and comforting.



Ingridients

  • Penne pasta (500g)

  • Tomato paste (2 tablespoons)

  • Cherry tomatoes (2 cups)

  • Garlic (3 cloves)

  • 1 piece of Onion

  • Butter (1 tablespoon)

  • Basil leaves(As much as preferred)

  • Chili flakes (2 teaspoons)

  • Cream fresh (350 grams)

  • Parmesan cheese (150 grams)

How to make Penne Rosé

  1. You begin to boil the pasta then set aside and take a cup of the pasta's water.

  2. In a separate pan, you saute the onion garlic chili flakes with butter, when cooked well you add the cherry tomato. Let the cherry tomato simmer for about 5 minutes then you add the tomato paste.

  3. You then add the pasta water that was reserved and then the cream. When cooked well add the pasta and parmesan cheese.
