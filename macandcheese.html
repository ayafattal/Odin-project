Mac and Cheese


An easy homemade Mac and Cheese recipe that will be tasty. Mac and Cheese is a rich and creamy dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with cheesy sauce.


Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni

  • 1/4 cup butter

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • Ground black pepper for the taste

  • 2 cups of milk

  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese

How to make homemade Mac and Cheese:

  1. Get a large pot and add water and a pinch of salt to boil. Cook the elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stir it for 8 minutes until its cooked.

  2. At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.

  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until it smooths for about 5 minutes.

  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling for about 5 minutes, make sure the milk doesn't burn.

  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.

  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until mixed properly.
