Fettuccine Alfredo


Fettuccine Alfredo is tender pasta in a luscious cream sauce with garlic and fresh parmesan cheese. A classic Italian American favorite, Alfredo pasta is easy to make at home with simple ingredients.


Beginner cooks may easily make this dish because it is so rich and creamy. This Pasta recipe is the real deal! Parmesan, butter, heavy cream, and pasta mixed right at the table, perfect for a family dinner or entertaining a crowd.


These are the ingredients you'll need to make fettuccine Alfredo:

  • Pasta: You will be needing fettuccine pasta.

  • Butter: For the Alfredo sauce you will need two sticks of butter.

  • Cream: For the sauce you will be needing one cup of heavy cream.

  • Seasonings: For the fettuccine Alfredo is simply seasoned with salt, pepper and garlic salt.

  • Cheese: You'll need Parmesan cheese.

How to make Fettuccine Alfredo:

  1. Use a large pot and add water with a pinch of salt to boil. Then add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.

  2. Melt the butter into the heavy cream in a large saucepan over low heat. Then you add salt, pepper and garlic salt. Increase the heat to medium and stir the grated parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce has thickened.

  3. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until its stirred properly then add basil leaves for the final touch. When done you can serve the dish.
